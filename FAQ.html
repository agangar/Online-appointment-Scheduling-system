Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)
Below is a list of frequently asked questions pertaining to the Appointment Scheduling System.If you need answers to questions not addressed on this page, please contact us at appointsys@gmail.com.

Q: How do I get started?

For Doctor: All you need is a computer/mobile that has an Internet browser installed. It's easy to set up your own online appointment scheduler - after you register with us our step-by-step account setup wizard will interview you about your specialization and on submit your account is created. 
For Patient: All you need is a computer/mobile that has an internet browser installed.Type your choice of doctor and your pincode.Select the appointment time and fill in your details.Your appointment is booked. 

Q:Once if I have booked the appoinment,can I cancel it?

Only a Doctor is allowed to cancel the appointment if there is some emergency.The Patient however cannot cancel the appointment after he has booked it.

Q:Will Appointment Scheduling System disclose my personal  and account details?

Keeping your personal and account related information private is vitally important to us.Appointment Scheduling System will never rent or sell your details to anyone.
